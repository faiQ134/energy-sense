Cutting Energy Use at Home

In Australia, appliance energy consumption can account for a sizable share of household electricity use. Look for the Energy Rating Label (stars + kWh per year) to compare products.

Quick wins

  • Choose high star-rating models (lower annual kWh).
  • Enable eco and auto standby modes on TVs and set-top boxes.
  • Use smart power boards to reduce idle/standby draw.

Televisions: Energy & Picture

TVs vary widely in energy use. Panel type (OLED, QLED/Mini-LED, LCD), size, brightness settings, and features (HDR, high refresh rate) all impact consumption.

Tips to cut TV energy

  • Turn on Ambient Light / Eco modes.
  • Reduce Backlight / OLED Light for night viewing.
  • Enable auto power-down after inactivity.
  • Consider screen size vs. seating distance (don’t oversize).
